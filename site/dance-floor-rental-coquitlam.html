Dance Floor Rentals Questions — Coquitlam

What size dance floor do I need for my Coquitlam event?
A general rule of thumb: ~4.5 square feet per dancer, assuming half your guests are dancing at once. A 12×12 floor works for about 50 guests; 15×15 for 100; 18×18 for 150; and 20×20 for 200+. For Coquitlam weddings, we'll help you right-size during booking.
Can you install the dance floor on grass or under a tent in Coquitlam?
Yes — our dance floor includes a subfloor system that levels over grass, carpet, uneven pavement, or existing venue floors. For Coquitlam outdoor weddings under a marquee tent, we stage the dance floor first, then install the tent around it.
How long does it take to install the dance floor?
For a standard 15×15 or 18×18 floor in Coquitlam, our crew needs roughly 60–90 minutes of clear floor time to install and level. We coordinate the delivery window with your venue so the floor is in place before florals, staging, and head table dressing.
Do you deliver party rentals to Coquitlam?
Yes — we deliver and set up throughout Coquitlam, including Town Centre, Westwood Plateau, and Burke Mountain. From our Surrey warehouse, it's about 35-45 minutes. Delivery and setup are included in every quote, with no surprise fees at the venue.
How far in advance should I book for an event in Coquitlam?
For peak season (May through September) in Coquitlam, we recommend booking 6–8 weeks out — especially for weekend weddings. Off-season and weekday events can often be booked 1–2 weeks out. You can check live availability and reserve with a 25% deposit on our online booking page 24/7.
