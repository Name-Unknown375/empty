Dance Floor Rentals Questions — New Westminster

What size dance floor do I need for my New Westminster event?
A general rule of thumb: ~4.5 square feet per dancer, assuming half your guests are dancing at once. A 12×12 floor works for about 50 guests; 15×15 for 100; 18×18 for 150; and 20×20 for 200+. For New Westminster weddings, we'll help you right-size during booking.
Can you install the dance floor on grass or under a tent in New Westminster?
Yes — our dance floor includes a subfloor system that levels over grass, carpet, uneven pavement, or existing venue floors. For New Westminster outdoor weddings under a marquee tent, we stage the dance floor first, then install the tent around it.
How long does it take to install the dance floor?
For a standard 15×15 or 18×18 floor in New Westminster, our crew needs roughly 60–90 minutes of clear floor time to install and level. We coordinate the delivery window with your venue so the floor is in place before florals, staging, and head table dressing.
