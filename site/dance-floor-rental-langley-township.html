What size dance floor do I need for my Langley Township event?
A general rule of thumb: ~4.5 square feet per dancer, assuming half your guests are dancing at once. A 12×12 floor works for about 50 guests; 15×15 for 100; 18×18 for 150; and 20×20 for 200+. For Langley Township weddings, we'll help you right-size during booking.
Can you install the dance floor on grass or under a tent in Langley Township?
Yes — our dance floor includes a subfloor system that levels over grass, carpet, uneven pavement, or existing venue floors. For Langley Township outdoor weddings under a marquee tent, we stage the dance floor first, then install the tent around it.
