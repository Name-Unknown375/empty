Party Rental Questions — Port Moody

Do you deliver party rentals to Port Moody?
Yes — we deliver and set up throughout Port Moody, including Moody Centre, Inlet Centre, and Heritage Woods. From our Surrey warehouse, it's about 40-50 minutes via Highway 1. Delivery and setup are included in every quote, with no surprise fees at the venue.
What's included in the delivery and setup fee?
Every rental includes round-trip delivery, professional setup of tents and dance floors, takedown, and pickup. Chairs and tables are stacked and placed where you want them. There are no hidden charges or fuel surcharges — the price you see at checkout is the price you pay.
How far in advance should I book for an event in Port Moody?
For peak season (May through September) in Port Moody, we recommend booking 6–8 weeks out — especially for weekend weddings. Off-season and weekday events can often be booked 1–2 weeks out. You can check live availability and reserve with a 25% deposit on our online booking page 24/7.
Have you worked at venues in Port Moody like Rocky Point Park?
Yes — we regularly deliver rentals to Rocky Point Park, Shoreline Trail, and Buntzen Lake and dozens of private residences across Port Moody. We know the setback rules for most public parks in Port Moody and can advise on what permits (if any) your venue requires for tents.
Can you set up a tent on grass, gravel, or a paved driveway?
Yes to all three. Our marquee tents use stake-down installation on grass and ballasted (weighted) installation on pavement, gravel, and stamped concrete. Tell us the surface when you book and we'll send the right hardware. We do not tent on artificial turf.
