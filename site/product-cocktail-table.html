Cocktail Highboy Table Rental

Starting from $15 / day

Highboy cocktail tables are the quiet superstar of any corporate or wedding reception — they create natural mingling spots, break up a room's sightlines, and give guests somewhere to park a drink without committing to a chair. We stock 42"-tall round highboys with folding legs for quick staging. Pair them with bistro lighting for an instant pre-dinner zone; most customers rent 1 highboy per 10-12 standing guests. Spandex covers available through our linen partners.
