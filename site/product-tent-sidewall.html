Tent Sidewall Rental

Every marquee we rent includes solid sidewalls as standard, but sidewalls can also be rented separately — for popup tents, for weather-swap panels, or for cathedral-window upgrades where you want natural light without exposing the space to wind. Panels clip cleanly to the frame; swap-in during install or mid-event if the weather changes. Available in solid white or cathedral-window style.
