FAQ

Resin Garden Chair Rental — Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Resin Garden chair and when should I choose it?

The Resin Garden chair (also called a white resin stacking chair) is an injection-moulded, UV-resistant chair built for high-volume outdoor events. Choose it when you need a large number of chairs at the most affordable per-chair price — backyard weddings, poolside celebrations, beach ceremonies, community events, church or school functions, and any gathering where budget-per-head is the deciding factor.

How is the Resin Garden chair different from the Fanback?

The Resin Garden chair has a solid, simple back panel and stacks 15 high (vs 10 for Fanback). It's our most budget-forward chair, best for events where cost-per-chair is the primary concern. The Fanback has a decorative fan-pattern back that reads as more formal. If you're running a large backyard party or community event and want the lowest per-chair cost, go Resin Garden. For outdoor weddings where aesthetics matter, Fanback or Chiavari are more popular. Mix-and-match is welcome — all styles deliver on the same truck at no mixing fee.

Are Resin Garden chairs suitable for outdoor weddings?

Yes, for the right setting. They work beautifully for casual or boho-style outdoor weddings, relaxed backyard ceremonies, and informal celebration formats. For formal white-tablecloth receptions, most clients upgrade to Chiavari chairs. A popular combination: Resin Garden chairs for the outdoor ceremony (volume, budget) and Chiavari chairs for the tented or indoor reception — all delivered on one truck, one order.

What is the weight capacity of the Resin Garden chair?

Our Resin Garden chairs are rated to 300 lb. If you have guests who may need higher-capacity seating, our Fanback and Chiavari chairs are both rated to 500 lb and can be mixed into the same order.

How many can I order and is there a minimum?

There is no minimum order. We supply Resin Garden chairs for events from 20 guests to 200+. They stack 15 high for efficient truck loading, so large orders are straightforward to deliver. For orders over 200 chairs, contact us to confirm availability on your specific date.

Do you clean and inspect chairs before delivery?

Yes — every chair is cleaned and inspected before each rental, regardless of style or price. Any chair showing visible cracking, staining, or structural wear is retired from the fleet before it reaches your event. You receive the same inspection standard as our higher-priced Chiavari chairs.

How far in advance should I book?

For summer events (May–September), 4–6 weeks ahead is recommended. For smaller or off-peak orders, 1–2 weeks is usually sufficient. Check live availability and hold your date with a 25% deposit via our online booking system at any time.
