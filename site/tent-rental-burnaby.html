Can you install a tent on grass, pavement, or a gravel driveway in Burnaby?
Yes to all three surfaces. For grass in Burnaby, we stake the tent down using engineered anchors. For pavement, stamped concrete, or driveways, we use ballasted weights (typically water barrels or concrete blocks) rated for the tent size and local wind conditions. Tell us the surface when you book and we'll dispatch the right hardware.
