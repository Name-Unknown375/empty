Can you install a tent on grass, pavement, or a gravel driveway in New Westminster?
Yes to all three surfaces. For grass in New Westminster, we stake the tent down using engineered anchors. For pavement, stamped concrete, or driveways, we use ballasted weights (typically water barrels or concrete blocks) rated for the tent size and local wind conditions. Tell us the surface when you book and we'll dispatch the right hardware.
