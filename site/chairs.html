FAQ

Chair Rental Questions

What chair styles do you offer?

We carry three chair styles: classic white Chiavari chairs with cushions (the top choice for weddings and galas), white Fanback folding chairs (elegant and practical for outdoor ceremonies), and white Resin Garden chairs (durable and weather-resistant for casual outdoor events).

How many chairs can you supply for one event?

We can handle orders from 10 chairs to 1,000+ chairs. Our fleet is large enough for full corporate events and large-scale weddings. For orders over 300 chairs, contact us directly to confirm availability and logistics.

Are Chiavari chairs comfortable for long events?

Yes — our Chiavari chairs come with soft cushions and are designed for all-day comfort. They're a favourite for weddings, galas, and corporate dinners where guests are seated for hours. Many clients tell us they're the most comfortable rental chairs they've experienced.

Do you deliver and arrange the chairs to our layout?

Absolutely. Our crew delivers, arranges chairs to your floor plan (ceremony rows, reception rounds, banquet style), and collects them after the event. Share your layout and headcount when booking and we'll handle everything.

Are the chairs suitable for outdoor events?

Yes. Our Resin Garden and Fanback chairs are particularly well-suited for outdoor use. Chiavari chairs work well outdoors too, especially under a tent or on a patio. We recommend a flat, stable surface for all chair types.

Is there a minimum chair order?

Our minimum order is 10 chairs. We also bring spare chairs to your event at no extra charge — just in case your guest count grows at the last minute.
