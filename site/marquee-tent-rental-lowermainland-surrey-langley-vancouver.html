Marquee Tent Rental Questions — Lower Mainland

Do you deliver marquee tents across the Lower Mainland?

Yes — we deliver, set up, and take down marquee tents throughout the Lower Mainland including Surrey, Langley, Vancouver, Burnaby, Richmond, Delta, Coquitlam, Maple Ridge, North Vancouver, and more. Delivery fees vary by distance from our Surrey warehouse at 9317 188 St.

How far in advance should I book a marquee tent rental?

For summer weddings and large outdoor events across the Lower Mainland, we recommend booking 3–6 months ahead. Peak season (May–September) fills quickly. For smaller or off-peak events, 4–6 weeks is usually sufficient. Check live availability and book online 24/7.

What sizes of marquee tents do you offer?

We offer four marquee tent sizes: 20×20 ft (up to 40 guests, from $550), 20×40 ft (up to 80 guests, from $1,100), 20×60 ft (up to 120 guests + dance floor, from $1,260), and 40×80 ft (250+ guests, from $2,400). All sizes are available throughout Surrey, Langley, Vancouver, and the broader Lower Mainland.

What does the tent rental include?

Every marquee tent rental includes delivery to your venue, full installation by our crew (frame assembly, staking or ballasting, canvas fitting), and post-event takedown and removal. Tables, chairs, and dance floors can be added to the same order.

Do marquee tents need to be staked into the ground?

On grass or soft soil we stake marquee tents for maximum stability. For concrete, asphalt, or paved surfaces we use heavy ballast water barrels as anchors. Both methods are fully secure — just let us know your surface type when booking.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer a 125% cancellation guarantee: if we ever cancel on you for any reason, we return 125% of every dollar paid. For client-initiated cancellations, your deposit is held as a credit toward a rescheduled date. See full terms at checkout.
