Party Rental Questions — Ladner

Do you deliver party rentals to Ladner?
Yes — we deliver and set up throughout Ladner, including Ladner Village, Port Guichon, and Holly. From our Surrey warehouse, it's about 20–30 minutes via Highway 99. Delivery and setup are included in every quote, with no surprise fees at the venue.
What's included in the delivery and setup fee?
Every rental includes round-trip delivery, professional setup of tents and dance floors, takedown, and pickup. Chairs and tables are stacked and placed where you want them. There are no hidden charges or fuel surcharges — the price you see at checkout is the price you pay.
How far in advance should I book for an event in Ladner?
For peak season (May through September) in Ladner, we recommend booking 6–8 weeks out — especially for weekend weddings. Off-season and weekday events can often be booked 1–2 weeks out. You can check live availability and reserve with a 25% deposit on our online booking page 24/7.
Have you worked at venues in Ladner like Westham Island?
Yes — we regularly deliver rentals to Westham Island, George C. Reifel Migratory Bird Sanctuary, and Millennium Park and dozens of private residences across Ladner. We know the setback rules for most public parks in Ladner and can advise on what permits (if any) your venue requires for tents.
Can you set up a tent on grass, gravel, or a paved driveway?
Yes to all three. Our marquee tents use stake-down installation on grass and ballasted (weighted) installation on pavement, gravel, and stamped concrete. Tell us the surface when you book and we'll send the right hardware. We do not tent on artificial turf.
What happens if the weather is bad or I need to cancel?
Our 125% Cancellation Protection means if we ever cancel your booking, you receive 125% of your deposit back. If you need to reschedule because of weather, we'll roll your deposit forward to a new date at no charge — as long as we're notified before crew dispatch. Cancellations initiated by the customer are subject to our standard deposit terms.
