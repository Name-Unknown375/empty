FAQ

Fanback Folding Chair Rental — Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a Fanback folding chair?

A Fanback chair is a white moulded-resin folding chair with a distinctive fan-shaped back panel. The fan pattern adds a decorative touch that makes it look more formal than a standard folding chair — it reads as a proper event chair outdoors while still folding completely flat for easy transport and storage. It's the most popular outdoor ceremony chair in the Lower Mainland.

How does the Fanback chair compare to the Chiavari?

The Fanback is our practical outdoor workhorse; the Chiavari is our formal reception standard. Fanback chairs fold flat (great for transport and self-setup), cost less per chair, and handle rough outdoor surfaces well. Chiavari chairs are non-folding, come with cushions, and photograph better at formal seated dinners. A common approach: Fanback chairs outside for the ceremony, Chiavari chairs inside for the reception — both delivered on one truck at no mixing surcharge.

Are Fanback chairs comfortable without cushions?

Yes for ceremony-length seating (30–60 minutes). The moulded seat has a slight contour that's comfortable for the duration of an outdoor ceremony. For receptions or events over 90 minutes where guests are seated the whole time, we recommend our Chiavari chairs with cushions instead — or ask us about adding a cushion option.

Can I use Fanback chairs on grass or uneven ground?

Yes — Fanback chairs are well-suited for grass, gravel, and mildly uneven outdoor surfaces. The four-leg base distributes weight evenly across the ground. On very soft or wet grass, legs may sink slightly — our crew will advise on spacing and alignment if needed. They're rated to 500 lb, so stability on solid ground is not a concern.

Can I pick up Fanback chairs myself instead of having them delivered?

Yes — Fanback chairs are fully foldable and available for self-pickup from our Surrey warehouse at 9317 188 St. They stack 10 high and fit in most SUVs, minivans, trucks, and cargo vans. Contact us ahead of time to arrange a pickup order and confirm availability on your date.

How many Fanback chairs can I order?

We carry 440 Fanback chairs — our highest-volume chair option. They're well-suited for large community events, outdoor weddings, graduations, and fundraisers of 100–400 guests. For orders over 400, contact us to confirm availability on your specific date.

How far in advance should I book?

For large summer events (100+ chairs, May–September), 6–8 weeks ahead is recommended. For smaller orders or off-peak dates, 2–4 weeks is usually sufficient. Check live availability and hold your date with a 25% deposit via our online booking system at any time.
