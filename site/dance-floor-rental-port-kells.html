What size dance floor do I need for my Port Kells event?
A general rule of thumb: ~4.5 square feet per dancer, assuming half your guests are dancing at once. A 12×12 floor works for about 50 guests; 15×15 for 100; 18×18 for 150; and 20×20 for 200+. For Port Kells weddings, we'll help you right-size during booking.
