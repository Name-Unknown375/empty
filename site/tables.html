FAQ

Table Rental Questions

What table sizes and styles do you carry?

We carry 8ft and 6ft rectangular (banquet) tables, 5ft round tables, and cocktail/high-top tables. All tables are pressure-washed and arrive spotlessly clean — pristine enough to use without tablecloths, though most clients add linens for formal events.

How many guests fit at each table type?

An 8ft banquet table comfortably seats 8–10 guests. A 6ft rectangular table seats 6–8. A 5ft round table seats 8 guests comfortably (up to 10 if needed). Cocktail/high-top tables are standing height and accommodate 4–6 people for mingling.

Can I mix table types in one order?

Absolutely — most events combine table types. Round tables for seated dinner, banquet tables for buffet lines, and cocktail tables for the reception entrance or bar area are a classic combination. We deliver and set up any mix in a single order.

Do you set up tables to our floor plan?

Yes. Our crew delivers and positions tables to your specified layout. Share your floor plan dimensions and we'll position everything before guests arrive. We also bring extras at no charge in case your count grows.

How many tables do I need for my guest count?

For seated dinners: divide your guest count by 8 for round tables, or by 10 for banquet tables. For example, 80 guests needs 10 round tables or 8 banquet tables. Add 1–2 extras for head tables, gifts, or buffet. Call us and we'll help you plan the exact count.

Is there a minimum table order?

There's no strict minimum — we accommodate orders starting at a handful of tables. Contact us for very small orders and we'll work out the best arrangement.
